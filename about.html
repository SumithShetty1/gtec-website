Who We Are

G-TEC Education is an ISO-certified global leader in IT education, skill development, and vocational training with 25+ years of excellence. With a strong network of 800+ training centres across 23+ countries, G-TEC delivers industry-focused courses and internationally recognized certifications.

G-TEC is an authorized training partner of leading organizations such as SAP, Tally, ZOHO, Microsoft, Adobe, EC-Council, and government bodies like NIELIT and Keltron. Through divisions like G-TEC Computer Education, G-TEC College of Advanced Studies, G-TEC Global Campus, and G-TEC Online Learning, we provide quality education, practical skills, and career support to help students succeed globally.
